About

Narratoons is an AI-powered web application that transforms your text descriptions and voice input into vibrant comic panels. Simply type or speak a scene, choose from Japanese, American, Egyptian, or Indian comic art styles, and select your preferred panel layout — Narratoons generates stunning visuals in seconds using Stability AI's image generation technology. Built with a FastAPI Python backend and a clean HTML/CSS/JavaScript frontend, the app supports three unique grid layouts to bring your stories to life in a structured comic format. Whether you're a storyteller, student, or creative enthusiast.
